## Welcome!
This is a mod that adds a LOT of new roles.

There is an ArtSwap folder attached containing the art I use for each role.

Currently, this mod includes the following roles:
#### Arbiter (Villager)
Pick 1 character:
Learn if they are Disguised.
#### Arithmetician (Villager)
Learn the sum of all Evil characters' positions.
#### Cardshark (Villager)
Pick 1 characters:
For each Villager picked, deal 1 damage to you.
For each non-Villagear picked, heal you for 1 health.
#### Chiromancer (Villager)
Learn 3 characters and which Evil role each is. Only 1 is correct.
#### Clairvoyant (Villager)
Learn 2 characters: Both are Good, or both are Evil.
#### Convict (Villager)
Pick 1 character:
They die, but don't reveal their True Role.
Learn their alignment.
#### Devout (Villager)
Game Start:
Reveal me.
Learn 1 Outcast character.
#### Forager (Villager)
Pick 1 character:
Learn if they are a Villager.
#### Gossip (Villager)
Pick 1 character:
Learn random info about them.
#### Introvert (Villager)
Learn 2 characters that sit within 2 seats of me.
#### Jewelsmith (Villager)
Learn 1 Honest character.
#### Lamb (Villager)
Learn how far from me to a particular Outcast.
#### Performer (Villager)
Learn 1 Evil character.
If I am adjacent to Evil, Learn false info.
#### Prince (Villager)
Learn that exactly 1 of 2 characters is Disguised.
#### Ranger (Villager)
Learn how far from me to my furthest Evil.
#### Scavenger (Villager)
Activate Me:
For every dead Evil character, gain 1 Max Health and Heal you for 2 health.
#### Sentinel (Villager)
Learn that exactly 1 of 2 characters is Corrupted.
#### Sheriff (Villager)
Learn 1 role that is being used as a Disguise.
#### Spy (Villager)
Learn an Evil role that has not been Revealed yet (or that all have been).
#### Warden (Villager)
Pick 4 characters:
Learn which character type is the most common among them.

#### Chatterbox (Outcast)
Reveal:
1 Unrevealed Good Villager becomes Corrupted, if possible.
#### Lunatic (Outcast)
I Disguise.
I have a 50% chance to be Corrupted.
#### Marionette (Outcast)
Game Start:
I sit next to the Demon, if possible.

I Register as a Puppet.
I Lie and Disguise.
#### Pilgrim (Outcast)
1 random character Disguises as the Pilgrim.
#### Revolutionary (Outcast)
On Death:
I Execute an alive Village, if possible.

I Disguise.
#### Renegade (Outcast)
I am Evil.
I Lie and Disguise.

#### Acolyte (Minion)
I am an Evil created by another character's ability.
I Lie and Disguise as an out-of-play character.
#### Professional (Minion)
I Lie and Disguise.
I Register as Good and as my Disguise.
#### Saboteur (Minion)
Game Start:
1 Good Villager furthest away from me is Corrupted.

I Lie and Disguise.
#### Swarm, Good (Minion)
Game Start:
2 other Good Villagers become Swarm. Both are Evil.

Learn 4 characters, 2 of which are Swarm.
#### Swarm, Evil (Minion)
Game Start:
2 other Good Villagers become Swarm. One is Good, one is Evil.

I Lie and Disguise.
#### Turncoat (Minion)
I Disguise.
#### Undying (Minion)
Game Start:
1 Good Villager might become an Acolyte

Execute Me:
If other Evil characters still live, I don't die; deal 4 damage to you.
#### Zealot (Minion)
Game Start:
I am an Evil created by another character's ability.
I Lie and Disguise as an in-play character.

#### Agmeres (Demon)
Setup:
Most characters are Minions.

Game Start:
You have 2 less Max Health.

At Night:
Deal 2 damage to you. Lose 2 Max Health.

Lose if all Good characters die, even if I'm dead.

I Lie and Disguise.
#### Caedoccidere (Demon)
At Night:
Kill 1 Revealed Good character.
Deal 3 damage to you.

I Lie and Disguise.
#### Emenverax (Demon)
1 Good Villager Disguises as Emenverax.
Take 3 extra damage if you Execute them.
#### Magnere (Demon)
Setup:
Villages are much bigger than usual.

Game Start:
Your Max Health is equal to the village size.
2 Demons are added to the Deck View; I become one of them.
#### Mendaverte
Good Villagers are Corrupted.
Evil characters tell the truth.
I Lie and Disguise.
#### Praesect (Demon)
Game Start:
2 Good Villagers become an Acolyte and a Zealot.

I Lie and Disguise.
#### Sanguitaurus (Demon)
Game Start:
You have more or less health depending on the size of the village.
Night takes half as long to fall.

At Night:
Kill & Reveal 1 Good character.
Deal 2 damage to you.

Lose if all Good characters die.

I Lie and Disguise.
#### Specularus (Demon)
Everyone Disguises, but nobody Lies.

Many fake Villagers and Outcasts are added to the Deck View.
#### Venelum (Demon)
Game Start:
1 Good Villager adjacent to me becomes Corrupted, if possible. They *cannot be Cured.*
1 Good Corrupted character becomes Evil.

I Lie and Disguise.
#### Veniyon (Demon)
Game Start:
Vidiyon is in-play.

We Lie and Disguise.
I Disguise like a Minion.
#### Vidiyon (Demon)
Game Start:
Reduce your Max Health by 2.

We Lie and Disguise.
#### Viciyon (Demon)
At Night:
Deal 1 damage to you.
If it's my first night, deal 2 instead.

We Lie and Disguise.
